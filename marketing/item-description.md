## Full description

### One `data-theme`. Ten completely different products.

**Decathemes** is a production UI kit for teams who need to ship fast _and_ look premium. Change a single HTML attribute and your dashboard, marketing site, auth flows, and storefront adopt a new **visual identity** — heading fonts, border radius, shadows, spacing, and color tokens together.

**See the proof in the live demo:** open `/capture/theme-identity-grid` on the preview site — the **same dashboard page** rendered in all 10 themes side by side. Brutalist (0px radius, offset shadows) and Playful (pill buttons, candy palette) are not the same product with different hex codes.

Built for the stack buyers use in 2026: **Next.js 16 App Router**, **React 19**, **TypeScript**, **Tailwind CSS v4**, **shadcn/ui** (new-york). Copy blocks, switch themes, deploy.

---

### The theme-switch moment (try this first)

1. Open the live preview → homepage hero or `/preview`
2. Switch themes with the top bar or ⌘K palette
3. Watch the **circular view-transition wipe** restyle the full UI
4. Compare **Minimal**, **Brutalist**, **Glass**, **Playful**, and **Editorial** — notice fonts and shape, not just color

That transition is what you ship to your users.
